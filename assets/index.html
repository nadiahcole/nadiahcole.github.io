My Dream Destinations

Athens Greece 

In Athens you can visit the Areopagus, the Ancient Agora, and nearby Byzantine churches to experience both Bible times and Christian history in one journey.

Italy

I want to taste Italian  food untouched by American influence . From handmade pasta to wood-fired pizza, I’m eager to experience dishes exactly as Italians have enjoyed them.
Italy’s museums are full of history and creativity, filled with masterpieces by Michelangelo, Leonardo da Vinci, Botticelli, and countless others. 

Ireland

Ireland is a dream destination for me because of its dramatic coastlines, mystical landscapes, and serene green countryside. If your focus is on nature, Ireland offers some of the most breathtaking and diverse scenery in Europe
